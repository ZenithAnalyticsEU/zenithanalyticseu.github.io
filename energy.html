Objectives

Our objective is to bring to the market information and communications technology (ICT)-based solutions towards raising energy efficiency and promoting use of renewable energy, as well as to provide smart meter analytics solutions that can serve energy providers, energy utilities or system operators.

Applications

Smart grid data analytics

Meeting the European Union energy and climate change objectives for 2020 requires large-scale integration of renewable energy sources and electric vehicles in distribution systems, while at the same time electricity markets call for higher flexibility and more efficient energy management. Many of the novel load monitoring and control functionalities can only be implemented within a smart grid environment and necessitate an advanced metering infrastructure. Smart meters present unprecedented opportunities to push the boundaries of grid visibility beyond substations and transformers and into the home, since it is now possible to view and analyze consumption data in new ways for a plethora of business applications, including among others load forecasting, demand management and peak power consumption reduction, capacity planning, exception analysis for preventing equipment failure, trending and correlation analysis. A smart meter analytics strategy provided by Zenith Analytics can identify the opportunities of smart meter data business applications and determine the supporting data requirements and the analytical processes needed to turn raw data into actionable information for improved decision-making, assisting organizations such as energy providers, energy utilities or system operators to attain the maximum benefits from smart meters.

ICT-based products for green awareness

The development of technologies which enable a reduction in energy required to provide products and services along with the exploitation of renewable energy sources are the two main pillars of sustainable energy policies. Technologies around green-tech and clean-tech have led to significant reductions in CO2 emissions and have increased energy efficiency, however the next big driver of innovation will be in the clean-web, which combines ICT enhancements with green initiatives. Meanwhile, environmental awareness is increasingly finding itself high among the priorities of businesses, driven by the consumersâ€™ demand for environmentally-friendly products and services. To mitigate the adverse impacts that businesses and their products and services have on the environment solutions which help reduce consumption of energy and raw materials, limit greenhouse gas emissions, minimize waste and pollution, and protect and restore ecosystems are highly promoted. Today businesses can benefit not only from investments in infrastructure to increase energy efficiency, but also from recent advancements in ICT used for employee-training and public information campaigns. Zenith Analytics provides innovative solutions to engage people, motivate actions and promote learning, supported upon the development of a set of serious games, social networking and personalized applications to foster co-creativity in learning processes in the context of both formal and informal educational settings. To this end, novel practices that fully integrate information collected from a set of sensor networks and mobile crowd sensing activities along with processes for monitoring, reporting and analysing sets of data with regards to energy consumption and behavioral profiles are being employed.
